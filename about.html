Meaganrose Pearsall

Art has always been a part of me, even when I tried to set it aside for a “real job.” After joining the military and finding a stable career, art came back into my life, and this time, I want to see where it takes me.

I'm 26, originally from Huntley, IL, but now I call Tucson, AZ, home, thanks to my military journey. It's where I met and married my husband, where we are both growing in our careers, and where we're raising our fur babies.

I have a Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, and my military experience has given me a diverse background in IT. I have combined my joy for creating art with my IT background and do all the website development for this site myself.
